Privacy Policy for Toori

Last updated: May 14, 2026

App name: Toori

Developer: Walter Unterberger

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Toori application ("the App") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the App.

Toori is an educational Android application designed to support Japanese language learning, including kana, kanji, vocabulary study, progress tracking, and related learning tools.

Your privacy and data security are taken seriously. The App only collects information necessary to provide its functionality and maintain user accounts.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

When you create an account or log in, the following information may be collected:

  • Email address
  • Full name, if provided during registration
  • Firebase user identifier (UID)
  • Authentication credentials managed securely through Firebase Authentication

Passwords are not stored directly by the App. Authentication is handled securely through Firebase Authentication.

2. Learning Progress and Study Data

The App may store learning-related information necessary to provide its educational functionality, including:

  • Learning progress
  • Kanji, kana, and vocabulary study progress
  • Study statistics, such as session counts and performance data
  • App usage timestamps related to learning activity

Some of this data may be stored locally on your device. If account synchronization is enabled, selected learning progress and statistics may also be stored securely using Firebase services.

3. Technical Information

The App may process limited technical information necessary for operation, security, reliability, and account functionality.

If crash reporting, analytics, or diagnostic services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy and the Google Play data safety information will be updated accordingly before such functionality is published.

How We Use Information

Information collected or processed by the App is used only for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain app functionality
  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To store and display learning progress
  • To synchronize learning progress across sessions where account sync is enabled
  • To support password reset and account recovery
  • To improve reliability and maintain the educational experience

The App does not use personal data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

User account data and synced progress data are stored using Google Firebase services. Some learning data may also be stored locally on the user's device.

Security measures include:

  • Encrypted network communication
  • Secure authentication through Firebase Authentication
  • Controlled access to stored user data

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect user information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Information may be processed by trusted service providers strictly for the purpose of operating the App, including:

  • Firebase, provided by Google LLC, for authentication and backend data storage

These services process data under their own privacy and security obligations.

Data may also be disclosed if required by law or to comply with legal obligations.

Data Retention

User data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the App's functionality or to comply with legal obligations.

Users may delete their account and associated synced data at any time.

Account Deletion

Users may delete their account directly inside the Toori application:

Profile → Settings → Delete Account

Deleting an account removes the user's authentication account, user profile information, and associated synced data stored for the account.

If you cannot access the App, you may request deletion by email.

Dedicated account deletion page: Delete Your Toori Account

User Rights

Depending on applicable laws, users may have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of their data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable

Requests can be made using the contact information provided in this Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and educational use. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate parental consent where required by applicable law.
